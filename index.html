Our Story: Weaving Dreams from Kashmir

For over three generations, our family has been dedicated to preserving the ancient art of Kashmiri shawl making. Nestled in the beautiful valleys of Kashmir, our artisans pour their hearts into every thread, creating masterpieces that carry centuries of tradition.

Each shawl we offer is a testament to the skill and dedication of our master craftsmen. From the sourcing of the finest Pashmina wool from Changthangi goats at 14,000 feet altitude to the intricate hand embroidery that can take months to complete, we ensure authenticity at every step.
